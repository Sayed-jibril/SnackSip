Frequently Asked Questions

What makes SnackSip different from regular cups?

SnackSip is the world's first dual-compartment cup with a built-in cutting blade. Unlike regular cups, it lets you carry both snacks and drinks simultaneously while enabling food prep on-the-go. Its ergonomic design is optimized for one-hand use, making it perfect for people with limited mobility.

Is SnackSip dishwasher safe?

Yes! All SnackSip components are top-rack dishwasher safe. For longevity, we recommend hand washing the blade attachment.

What materials are used in SnackSip?

We use premium BPA-free Tritan plastic for the cup, food-grade silicone for seals, and stainless steel for the blade. All materials meet FDA and EU food safety standards.

How does the return/reward program work?

Return your used SnackSip to any partner location or via mail. After professional cleaning and inspection, you'll receive reward points redeemable for discounts on future purchases or donations to our community programs.

When will the hot drink version be available?

Our insulated hot drink model is currently in testing and expected to launch Q1 2024. Join our mailing list for updates!
