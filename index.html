Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SnackSip

What materials are used in SnackSip?

All SnackSip products are made from premium BPA-free, food-grade plastic that's durable and safe for both hot and cold contents. The built-in blade is made from stainless steel.

Is SnackSip dishwasher safe?

Yes! All components of SnackSip are dishwasher safe. We recommend placing it on the top rack for best results.

How do I clean the built-in blade?

The blade can be easily cleaned with warm soapy water. A small brush is included with your purchase to help clean hard-to-reach areas. Always ensure the blade is completely dry before storing.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not completely satisfied with your SnackSip. Simply contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You'll see the exact shipping costs at checkout before completing your purchase.
